Infectious-diseases

Infectious diseases are caused by germs or microorganisms which are likely to be bacteria or fungi. Infectious diseases are the world's most dangerous killers. And to prevent those understanding the causal agents, risk factors, and situations that contribute to a certain illness is important. Additionally, knowing about the diseases can also stop them from infecting you and the people around you.
